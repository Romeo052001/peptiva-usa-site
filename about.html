Our mission

We make clinical peptides simple to deliver

Peptide therapy is one of the fastest-growing categories in aesthetics and longevity — but most practices still rely on compounded vials and home-mixing. Peptiva USA removes that friction with a pre-filled, standardized pen your team can trust, visit after visit.

Standardized, not improvised

The science was never the obstacle. The workflow was.

Compounded workflows put the burden on the practice: reconstitution, storage, labeling, dosing math and contamination risk every time a vial is pierced. We ship a sealed, documented format that's ready for the patient the moment it leaves the package — so dosing is repeatable across every staff member and every appointment.

Every batch is cGMP-manufactured, third-party tested for identity and purity, and shipped with a Certificate of Analysis. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, because it's the standard your patients deserve.
