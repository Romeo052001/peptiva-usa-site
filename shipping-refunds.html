Shipping & Returns

Last updated: July 2026

Peptiva USA ships to verified clinical practices using appropriate handling for temperature-sensitive materials.

Shipping

Orders dispatch after practice verification and payment confirmation. Temperature-sensitive formats ship with cold-chain packaging. Transit times and cut-offs are shared at checkout and in your account.

Returns

Due to the nature of clinical and research materials, opened or temperature-compromised units cannot be returned. Report any damaged or incorrect shipments within 48 hours of delivery for review.

COA & batch matching

Every unit carries a batch number matched to its Certificate of Analysis. Contact your account rep for COA copies.
